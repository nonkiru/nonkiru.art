Summary

Estelle is a confident rookie Lancer pilot! She's very social and confident, taking on a leader role naturally.
Estelle, unknown to even herself, is stuck in a time loop with her Lich. She infrequently hears whispers from - what she assumes - is her Lich, warning her of the future. Whenever Estelle would die, she is instead sent back to the beginning of the loop. She eventually, does break this loop, upon learning of her past.

On Donnie Linne...

Donnie is the mother of Estelle. They were a part of a very short campaign with my girlfriend and her family. Because of that, there isn't too much to Donnie!
In short, Donnie was a part of a very famous and wealthy family - and she stole their prized Emperor mech to pilot.
Donnie met Etch while on a job, and they had a child together. They were protective over Estelle due to Donnie being a wanted figure.
